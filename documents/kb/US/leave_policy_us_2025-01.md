## 1. Sick leave

- Full-time employees receive **10 days** of paid sick leave per year.
- Sick leave can be used for the employeeâ€™s illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member.

## 2. Parental leave

- Eligible full-time employees receive **12 weeks** of paid parental leave for birth or adoption.
- Parental leave must be requested at least **30 days** in advance when possible.

## 3. Jury duty

- Employees will receive paid time off for jury duty, subject to providing documentation.

## 4. Unpaid leave and protected leave

- Certain leaves may be protected by applicable law (for example, family/medical leave).
- HR will review eligibility and required documentation on a case-by-case basis.
