## 1. Health insurance enrollment

- New hires must enroll within **30 days** of hire date.
- Coverage begins on the **1st of the month** following enrollment, unless otherwise required by law.

## 2. Dental and vision

- Dental and vision are optional elections during open enrollment or a qualifying life event.

## 3. Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

- Annual contribution elections can be changed only during open enrollment or with a qualifying life event.
- Unused FSA funds follow the plan’s carryover rule (if available).

## 4. 401(k)

- Employees are eligible to contribute starting the first payroll after hire.
- Company match: **50% of employee contributions up to 4% of eligible pay**.

## 5. Qualifying life events (QLE)

Examples include:
- Marriage/divorce
- Birth/adoption of a child
- Change in spouse’s employment/coverage

QLE changes must be submitted within **30 days** of the event date.
