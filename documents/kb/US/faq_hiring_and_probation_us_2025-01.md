## What is the probation period?

The probation period for new hires is **90 days** from the start date. During this time, employees receive standard benefits unless otherwise specified in their offer letter.

## When do benefits begin?

Benefits enrollment is available within **30 days** of hire. Coverage begins on the **1st of the month** following enrollment.

## When can I request PTO?

PTO accrues biweekly and can be used after it has accrued. Submit PTO requests at least **3 business days** in advance when possible.
