## 1. Working hours and attendance

- Standard workweek is **40 hours**, Mondayâ€“Friday.
- Non-exempt employees must record time worked in the timekeeping system.
- Overtime must be **pre-approved by a manager**.

## 2. Paid time off (PTO)

### 2.1 Accrual

- Full-time employees accrue **15 days** of PTO per year (prorated by start date).
- PTO accrues biweekly and can be used after accrual posts.

### 2.2 Carryover

- Up to **5 days** of unused PTO may carry over into the next calendar year.
- Carryover beyond 5 days requires manager approval and HR review.

### 2.3 Requesting PTO

- Submit PTO requests at least **3 business days** in advance when possible.
- Managers may deny requests due to business needs.

## 3. Remote work policy (summary)

- Eligible roles may work remotely up to **3 days/week** with manager approval.
- Employees must maintain a secure workspace and follow confidentiality rules.

## 4. Code of conduct (summary)

- Treat colleagues with respect; harassment and discrimination are prohibited.
- Report concerns to HR or through the ethics hotline.
