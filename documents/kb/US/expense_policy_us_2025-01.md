## 1. General rule

- Business expenses must be **reasonable**, **necessary**, and **pre-approved** when required.
- Submit expense reports within **30 days** of the expense date.

## 2. Meals

- Meals are reimbursable when traveling or when hosting a business meeting.
- Alcohol is reimbursable only with client meals and requires manager approval.

## 3. Travel

- Economy airfare is standard unless otherwise approved.
- Use company-preferred vendors when available.

## 4. Non-reimbursable items

- Personal expenses
- Traffic fines
- Home office purchases without approval
